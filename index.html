Tame Impala is an Australian musical project led by instrumentalist and singer, Kevin Parker. Parker writes, records, produces, and performs all of the music. When touring, Tame Impala consists of Kevin Parker, Dominic Simper, Jay Watson, Cam Avery, and Julien Barbagallo. Most of the band's influence comes from 60s and 70s psychedelic rock and electronic music. As of now, they have four released albums: Innerspeaker (2010), Lonerism (2012), Currents (2015), and The Slow Rush (2020).
